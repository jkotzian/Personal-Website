Pokemon Red Remake

        Relive the classic Pokemon game from your childhood! Start your adventure all over again, this time with a new ultimate attack added to the battle system to shake things up.

        I made this along with Daniel Rings in the game development class at the University of Michigan. The goal of the assignment was to replicate the original game (up to the first couple towns) as functionally accurate as possible. Then, we had to add a new mechanic. We implemented an ultimate attack system that actually re-balances the original and (in our opinion) makes for a better experience. The game was made in Unity.
